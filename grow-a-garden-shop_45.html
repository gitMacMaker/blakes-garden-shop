✦ FAQ ✦
Is this safe? How do I know I won't get scammed?⌄
All trades are done manually by Blake in-game. Payment is required upfront, and Blake will find you in Roblox and send the items directly. If you're unsure, start with a cheap seed like Bamboo ($0.05) to build trust first.
How fast will I get my seeds?⌄
Usually within minutes of payment being confirmed. Blake will reach out via the chat and find you in Roblox as soon as possible. Make sure you're online and in-game. Response times may vary if Blake is offline.
What if I don't receive my item?⌄
If Blake can't find you in-game, he'll reach out via the chat to arrange another time. Make sure your Roblox username is spelled correctly. If there's a genuine issue, contact Blake on Discord (@eclipsekw) and it will be sorted out.
Can I buy multiple different seeds in one order?⌄
Currently each order is per item. You can buy multiple quantities of the same seed using the +/- selector. For mixed orders, just place multiple orders or message Blake in the chat and he can combine them into one trade.
Are refunds available?⌄
All sales are final once a trade has been sent. Mystery boxes are non-refundable by nature. If you paid but the trade was never delivered, contact Blake on Discord (@eclipsekw) and he will make it right.
